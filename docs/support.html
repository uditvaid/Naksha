Common Questions

How do I reset my birth data?
Go to Profile tab > scroll to bottom > tap "Reset Profile".

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone > tap your name > Subscriptions > Naksha > Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Profile tab > tap "Restore Purchases".

Is my data stored on a server?
No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.
